My name is Maryem, and I am engaged in the realm of Management Information Systems (MIS) at Saint Paul College. Outside the digital landscape, you can find me immersed in exploring the intersections of technology and business, constantly seeking opportunities to apply my MIS knowledge in real-world scenarios.
